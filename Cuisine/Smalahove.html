How to Prepare Smalahove

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheep's head, cleaned and prepared
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Potatoes, for serving
  • Rutabaga, for serving
  • Traditional flatbread (optional)

Cultural Significance

Smalahove has deep roots in Norwegian tradition, often served during special occasions such as Christmas or as part of local festivals. Its preparation and consumption are significant cultural events, celebrating regional heritage.
