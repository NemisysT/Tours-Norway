Simplified Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g mixed ground beef and pork
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg, and ginger to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Shape into oval meatballs.
  3. Fry in a pan until browned and cooked through.
  4. Serve with gravy, mashed potatoes, and lingonberry jam.

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
