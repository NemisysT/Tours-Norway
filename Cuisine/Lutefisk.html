Lutefisk: A Norwegian Delicacy

Simplified Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 dried cod, soaked and prepared
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare lutefisk as directed by soaking and rehydrating.
  2. Bake or boil the fish until tender.
  3. Serve with boiled potatoes, green peas, and melted butter.

Cooking Time: 40 minutes
