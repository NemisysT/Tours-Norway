Explore Norway: Whale Watching Adventures

Discover the majestic giants of the Norwegian seas

Andenes Whale Safari

Embark on an exciting whale watching tour from the picturesque town of Andenes.

Join expert guides on a thrilling whale watching expedition from Andenes, located in the Vesterålen archipelago. This area is known for its rich marine life and high success rate for whale sightings.

  • Best Time: May to September
  • Duration: 3-5 hours
  • Species: Sperm whales, orcas (seasonal), humpback whales

The tour includes a visit to the Whale Centre museum, where you can learn about the biology and conservation of these magnificent creatures.

Tromsø Orca Safari

Witness the majestic orcas in their natural habitat near Tromsø.

Experience the thrill of seeing orcas (killer whales) in the wild on this specialized safari from Tromsø. These intelligent and social creatures often visit the fjords around Tromsø to feed on herring.

  • Best Time: November to January
  • Duration: 5-8 hours
  • Includes: Warm suits, hot drinks, and light refreshments

This tour offers a unique opportunity to observe orcas and other whale species in the stunning Arctic scenery. Knowledgeable guides provide insights into the behavior and ecology of these fascinating marine mammals.

Lofoten Islands Whale Expedition

Combine whale watching with the breathtaking scenery of the Lofoten Islands.

Embark on a unique whale watching expedition in the stunning Lofoten archipelago. This tour combines the thrill of whale spotting with the awe-inspiring landscapes of one of Norway's most picturesque regions.

  • Available: Year-round (peak season: October to January)
  • Duration: Full day (6-8 hours)
  • Highlights: Whale watching, scenic fjords, traditional fishing villages

In addition to whales, you may spot other marine life such as sea eagles, seals, and various seabirds. The dramatic mountains and charming fishing villages of Lofoten provide a spectacular backdrop for this unforgettable adventure.
