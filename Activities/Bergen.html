Explore Bergen: Popular Activities

Discover the best of Norway's gateway to the fjords

Bryggen

Explore the historic Hanseatic wharf, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Bryggen, the old wharf of Bergen, is a reminder of the town's importance as part of the Hanseatic League's trading empire from the 14th to the mid-16th century. The colorful wooden buildings on Bryggen have been ravaged by many fires, the last in 1955, but its main structure has been preserved. Many of the buildings now house museums, shops, restaurants and pubs.

  • History: Dating back to the 14th century, Bryggen showcases Bergen's rich maritime heritage.
  • Architecture: Colorful wooden buildings line the old wharf, creating a picturesque scene.
  • Activities: Explore museums, artisan workshops, and enjoy local cuisine in historic surroundings.

A visit to Bryggen is a journey through time and a must-see for anyone visiting Bergen!

Mount Fløyen

Take the Fløibanen funicular for panoramic views of Bergen and the fjords.

Mount Fløyen is one of Bergen's most popular attractions. The Fløibanen funicular takes you to the top in about 8 minutes, where you can enjoy spectacular views of Bergen and the surrounding fjords and mountains.

  • Funicular Ride: A scenic journey to the top of Mount Fløyen, offering breathtaking views.
  • Hiking Trails: Explore numerous trails suitable for all skill levels.
  • Facilities: Restaurant, cafe, and a playground for children at the summit.

Perfect for nature lovers and those seeking panoramic views of Bergen and its beautiful surroundings!

Fish Market

Visit the lively fish market and taste fresh seafood from the Norwegian coast.

Bergen's Fish Market is one of Norway's most visited outdoor markets. Located in the heart of the city, it has been a meeting place for merchants and fishermen since the 1200s.

  • Fresh Seafood: Wide variety of fish and seafood caught from the Norwegian coast.
  • Local Produce: Fruits, vegetables, and other local specialties available.
  • Dining: Several eateries where you can enjoy freshly prepared seafood dishes.

A paradise for food lovers and a great place to experience local culture and cuisine!
