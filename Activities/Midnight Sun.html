Explore Norway: Midnight Sun Adventures

Experience the magic of endless daylight in the Land of the Midnight Sun

North Cape Midnight Sun

Witness the midnight sun at Europe's northernmost point.

The North Cape offers one of the most spectacular views of the midnight sun. Standing on the 307-meter high cliff, you can watch the sun skirt the horizon without setting.

  • Best Time: May 14 to July 29
  • Location: Nordkapp, Finnmark
  • Activities: Photography, nature walks, visitor center

Don't miss the opportunity to take in this awe-inspiring natural phenomenon at one of Europe's most iconic locations.

Lofoten Islands Midnight Golf

Play a round of golf under the midnight sun in the scenic Lofoten archipelago.

Experience the unique thrill of golfing at midnight with the sun still high in the sky. The Lofoten Links offers a spectacular 18-hole course surrounded by ocean views and rugged mountains.

  • Best Time: Late May to mid-July
  • Location: Gimsøysand, Lofoten
  • Activities: Golf, photography, hiking

This once-in-a-lifetime experience combines the beauty of the Arctic landscape with the joy of golf in surreal lighting conditions.

Tromsø Midnight Sun Cruise

Sail the Arctic waters and enjoy the midnight sun from the sea.

Embark on a magical cruise around Tromsø, known as the "Paris of the North," and witness the midnight sun reflecting off the calm Arctic waters.

  • Best Time: May 20 to July 22
  • Duration: 3-4 hours
  • Activities: Sightseeing, photography, wildlife spotting

This cruise offers a unique perspective of the midnight sun phenomenon, with opportunities to spot local wildlife and enjoy the stunning coastal scenery.
